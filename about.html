About ClimaSense

ClimaSense is a modern weather and air quality dashboard designed to provide you with real-time weather updates and air quality information for any city around the world.

  • Get current temperature, humidity, wind, and more.
  • Check the Air Quality Index (AQI) for your location.
  • Easy-to-use interface with responsive design.

This project is built using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, and fetches data from reliable weather and AQI APIs.

Created by Mohan Krishna Reddy.
